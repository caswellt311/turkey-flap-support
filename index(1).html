How to play

  • Tap anywhere to flap. Each tap gives the turkey a lift.
  • Fly through the gaps between fence posts. One point per post cleared.
  • Fly through a gravy boat for three bonus points.
  • Hitting a post or the ground ends the run. Tap to try again.

Frequently asked

Why does the game speed up when I grab gravy?

That's intentional. Difficulty scales with your score, and gravy boats add three points. Every gravy boat is a bargain: more points now, a faster game a moment later. Skipping one is a legitimate strategy.

What are the medals?

Bronze at 10 points, silver at 20, gold at 30, platinum at 40. They appear on the game over screen.

Is there something at 87 points?

Four score and seven. Get there and find out.

Why Abraham Lincoln?

Lincoln issued the 1863 proclamation that made Thanksgiving a national holiday. It seemed only right to have him on the sidelines.

I don't feel any vibration when I tap.

Check that Settings → Sounds & Haptics → System Haptics is turned on. Haptics are also disabled while Low Power Mode is active.

There's no sound.

Make sure your ring/silent switch isn't set to silent, and check the volume. You can also mute the game with the speaker button in the top right corner.

I lost my high score.

Your best score is stored only on your device. Deleting and reinstalling the app clears it, and it does not sync between devices or through iCloud.
