Privacy Policy

Turkey Flap · Last updated July 8, 2026

The short version: Turkey Flap collects nothing. No accounts, no analytics, no tracking, no ads, no network connection of any kind.

Information we collect

None. Turkey Flap does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. The app has no login, no user accounts, and makes no network requests. It works entirely offline.

Information stored on your device

The app saves one thing locally on your iPhone: your highest score. This number never leaves your device, is not associated with you or any identifier, and is not transmitted anywhere. Deleting the app removes it.

Third parties

Turkey Flap contains no third-party analytics, advertising networks, crash reporters, or software development kits that collect data. No data is shared with anyone, because none is gathered.

Children's privacy

Turkey Flap is rated suitable for ages 4 and up. Because the app collects no data whatsoever, it collects no data from children. It complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) by design.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated date. Any future version that collects data would be described here before that version is released.
